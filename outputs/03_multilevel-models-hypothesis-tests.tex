\hypertarget{purpose-and-hypotheses}{%
\section{1. Purpose and hypotheses}\label{purpose-and-hypotheses}}

This document runs the \textbf{main confirmatory analyses} for:

\begin{itemize}
\tightlist
\item
  \textbf{H1.} Higher right‐authoritarian / pro-Nazi attitudes predict
  \textbf{higher HPT scores} on the original instrument (risk of
  ideological contamination). Predictors: FR-LF-mini (total or RD/NS
  facets) and KSA-3.
\item
  \textbf{H2.} The H1 effect \textbf{persists controlling} for prior
  knowledge (KN total) and social desirability (SDR-5).
\end{itemize}

Notes on constructs and scoring:

\begin{itemize}
\tightlist
\item
  HPT subscores (POP, ROA, CONT) follow Hartmann \& Hasselhorn / Huijgen
  et al.~We treat \textbf{POP items as presentist} and therefore
  \textbf{reverse-score POP} so that \textbf{higher = more
  contextualised reasoning}. DVs used here are \textbf{HPT total (CTX6:
  POP\_rev+CONT)}, \textbf{CONT}, and \textbf{POP\_rev}.
\item
  FR-LF-mini uses \textbf{RD1--RD3} and \textbf{NS1--NS3}; we analyse
  \textbf{total} and \textbf{RD/NS facets}.
\item
  KSA-3 (9 items; aggression, submission, conventionalism) is included
  as a convergent authoritarian predictor. (Registered.)
\end{itemize}

\hypertarget{model-plan}{%
\section{3. Model plan}\label{model-plan}}

We estimate \textbf{random‐intercept multilevel models} with \textbf{two
clustering terms} (students nested in classes within schools):

\begin{itemize}
\tightlist
\item
  Base (FR-LF total):
  \texttt{DV\ \textasciitilde{}\ z\_frlf\_tot\ +\ z\_ksa3\_tot\ +\ z\_kn\_total\ +\ z\_sdr5\_tot\ +\ (1\ \textbar{}\ school\_id)\ +\ (1\ \textbar{}\ class\_id)}
\item
  Facet (RD/NS):
  \texttt{DV\ \textasciitilde{}\ z\_frlf\_rd\ +\ z\_frlf\_ns\ +\ z\_ksa3\_tot\ +\ z\_kn\_total\ +\ z\_sdr5\_tot\ +\ (1\ \textbar{}\ school\_id)\ +\ (1\ \textbar{}\ class\_id)}
\item
  Interaction (if preregistered):
  \texttt{DV\ \textasciitilde{}\ z\_frlf\_tot\ *\ z\_kn\_total\ +\ z\_ksa3\_tot\ +\ z\_sdr5\_tot\ +\ (1\ \textbar{}\ school\_id)\ +\ (1\ \textbar{}\ class\_id)}
\end{itemize}
